## 📖 Introduction | 项目简介
**SBTI Personality Test** is a professional, high-performance Single Page Application (SPA) designed to provide users with a deep dive into their personality based on a 15-dimension psychological model.  

**SBTI 人格测试** 是一款专业、高性能的单页应用（SPA）。它基于 15 维度心理模型，旨在为用户提供深度的自我认知分析。项目支持中英双语，具备极佳的用户体验与传播特性。

### ✨ Key Features | 核心特性
*   **Multilingual Support**: Seamless switching between English and Chinese. | **多语言支持**：中英双语完美切换。
*   **Professional Branding**: Branded social sharing icons for X, Facebook, Reddit, and Weibo. | **专业分享组件**：内置 X, Facebook, Reddit, 微博等热门社媒分享。
*   **High Performance**: Zero-dependency Vanilla JS/CSS architecture. | **极高性能**：原生代码开发，无框架依赖，秒开体验。
*   **SEO Ready**: Optimized meta tags, JSON-LD structured data, and Google Analytics. | **SEO 优化**：预设 Meta 标签、JSON-LD 结构化数据及 Google 统计。
